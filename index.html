Mobile Networking, Analytics and Edge Intelligence

The 6th Finland-China-Germany workshop on mobile networking, analytics and edge inetlligence is a two-day workshop jointly organized by the University of Helsinki, Tsinghua University, TU Delft, TU Munich, and Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. The workshop is intended to bring together professors, researchers and graduate students to share ideas, experience and implementations related to mobile networking, data analytics, edge intelligence and communication.

Host

Tsinghua University, Beijing, China

Topics

The workshop will cover following areas:

  • Intelligent wireless network
  • Intelligent technology and applications
  • Security and privacy
  • Data mining
  • Recommender system
  • Edge intelligence
